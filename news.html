Rahasya – A New Indian Horror Experience

We’re excited to share a first look at Rahasya, our upcoming horror game set in an old Indian haveli in 1992. This project is close to our hearts, built around the atmosphere and tension we’ve grown up hearing in family stories and local folklore.

Rahasya doesn’t focus on loud scares or exaggerated moments. Instead, it pulls you into a quiet, uneasy world where the house feels alive and every sound keeps you alert. The experience is designed to feel grounded, personal, and deeply Indian.

We’re putting a lot of attention into sound, visuals, and mood to make the haveli feel real — and the mystery inside it stay with you long after you stop playing.

This is just the beginning. More updates will follow as development progresses.
