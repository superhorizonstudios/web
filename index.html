The Story

The Secret
Buried Deep

In the heart of the Deccan plateau, an archaeologist named Aryan uncovers an ancient inscription that should not exist — a message written in a language that predates all known civilizations by millennia.

Rahasya (meaning "Secret" in Sanskrit) is a cinematic mystery thriller where you follow Aryan's descent into obsession, uncovering a conspiracy that spans centuries and threatens to unravel the very fabric of modern civilization.

Your choices determine how deep the rabbit hole goes — and whether Aryan emerges from the darkness, or becomes consumed by it.

"Some secrets were buried not because they were forgotten — but because finding them would destroy everything we believe to be true."

— Rahasya, Chapter I

Gameplay

Core Features

I

Investigation System

Collect evidence, analyze clues, and build your case. The game tracks every piece of information Aryan uncovers — including the things you miss.

C

Choice & Consequence

Every dialogue, every decision, every path taken cascades into the story. Four radically different endings based on your moral compass.

L

Living World Lore

Thousands of years of hidden history waiting to be uncovered. Books, artifacts, glyphs, and whispered conversations — the world is the story.

P

Puzzle Design

Ancient mechanisms, cryptographic ciphers, and logic puzzles rooted in real Indian mathematics and astronomy. No hand-holding. Pure discovery.

A

Atmospheric Audio

A full orchestral score blended with ambient environmental sound design. The music changes dynamically as your investigation deepens.

V

Cinematic Visuals

Built on Unity with Unity HDRP global illumination. Ancient ruins look and feel alive — shadows, dust, firelight all rendered in real time.

Our Story

About Super-
horizon

Superhorizon Studios was born from a simple but powerful idea: that games can be more than entertainment. They can be art. They can provoke emotion, challenge perception, and tell stories that matter.

Founded by a passionate team of creators, artists, and engineers, we are dedicated to crafting immersive experiences that push the boundaries of what games can be.

Our debut title, Rahasya, is more than a game — it's a statement. A declaration that independent studios can compete at the highest level of storytelling and visual craft.

Journey

Our Timeline

Early 2024
The Idea Begins

The concept for Rahasya is born from a fascination with Indian mythology and unsolved mysteries. First design documents drafted.

Late 2024
Superhorizon Studios Founded

The studio is officially established. Core team assembled. Mission defined: craft cinematic, story-driven games that leave a mark.

Early 2025
Rahasya Pre-Production

Narrative design locked. Level design prototypes created. Visual identity and art direction finalized. Unity pipeline set up.

2025 — Ongoing
Full Production Begins

Rahasya enters full development. Website launches. Community outreach begins. Demo planned for 2026.

Philosophy

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To become one of the most respected independent game studios in the world — known not just for technical excellence, but for games that genuinely move people. We believe the next great leap in gaming is emotional, not graphical.

Our Mission

To create immersive, story-driven experiences rooted in rich culture, authentic emotion, and design precision. Every game we ship will be a complete artistic statement — no rushed releases, no feature bloat. Quality over everything.
